Who am I?

Ever since I was a kid, I always had a curiousity towards softwares and computers. Now that I know a thing or two about making them, I am excited to contribute to the industry and to the people, using the skills that I have acquired.

Proficient in C/C++, Python, HTML, CSS and, familiar with Java, JavaScript and SQL. Worked on linux and windows environment. In other skills, I am acquainted with git, REST API, Django, STL, Cryptography libraries like GMP and PBC, etc.
